Tolls suck.
We're here to fix them.

tolls.io began as a hackathon project aimed at simplying the car tolling process.


There's a lot of problems with the tolling system today. Buying a new transponder in each state


is a drag. Making payments online is painfully counter-intuitive and slow. We hope to fix


all that with our all-in-one mobile application.
